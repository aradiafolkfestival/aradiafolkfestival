Manifesto


1.
Aradia is about creating an atmosphere centered on non-oppression and the rejection of anthropocentrism. A three-day folk festival to explore practices away from the logic of power.


2.
Aradia is discovery of self, place, and others, understood as a group within which to embark on a journey and to be able to imagine building connections and alliance relationships.


3.
Aradia is also synonymous with the grotesque, the playful, the unusual. Starting with elements rooted in the Italian village tradition, it emphasizes and invites a more direct, sincere and honest attitude with the context and with others.


4.
Aradia is a cyclical event and like any living being, it changes shape over time: each year it takes place in small places characterized by a traditional and folkloric matrix, far and uncontaminated from the city-system.
